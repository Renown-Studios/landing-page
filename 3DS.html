How to mod your New Nintendo 2DS XL or 3DS

You will need to start by downloading Luma3DS and boot9strap. I have linked a folder containing all the necessary files and I regularly update them with each new release.

Luma3DS + b9s (finaliser included)

  1. Now you have downloaded that file, place all the files, merging them if required, onto your SD card. Place the SD card into your console, and turn the console on. Everything will seem normal. Open your web browser and ensure you have WiFi access; The hack will not work otherwise. Then go to the url 'http://www.zoogie.github.io/super'. Then press 'GO GO!. Your console should freeze for a few seconds then open the b9s safe installer.
  2. Follow the prompts on screen, then select 'Scripts', then 'finalise'. Your console will reboot and then you are almost done!
  3. Open Rosalina Menu by holding down X + Y, and select 'Scripts' and select 'finalise' again. Then turn off your console. You are officially modded! It is recommended you backup your NAND as well, details can be found on '3ds.hacks.guide'.
