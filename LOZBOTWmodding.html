How to mod the Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

  1. We need to load our version of the game into CEMU to create the proper folders. Obtaining a copy of the game you do not own is ILLEGAL, please go to a third-party store to purchase a game disc of Breath of the Wild. Look at 'wiiu.hacks.guide' to find out how you can dump your game.
  2. Then, close the game. For this guide, I will be using the mod knon as Relics of tne Past. Download at 'relicsofthepast.dev'.
  3. Unzip the folder you have just installed, and open a new window of file explorer and navigate your way to the CEMU directory.
  4. Place the contents of 'rotpCEMU' into the 'graphicsPacks->breathOftheWild'. Relaunch CEMU and go to graphics packs and enable 'Relics of the Past'. You have modded the game successfully.
